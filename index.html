01About

A bit about the work

I've spent the last three years at Nirmaan Organization, working across different parts of communications rather than sticking to one lane. Some weeks that means writing a donor recognition post, others it's a video script, a press release, or content for an internal presentation.

Most of what I do involves taking programme work happening on the ground, in education, skilling, livelihoods, women's empowerment, sustainability, disaster relief, and rural development, and turning it into something a donor, a partner, or a volunteer can read and understand without needing context.

Day to day, that means working closely with programme teams, corporate partners, leadership, volunteers, beneficiaries, and designers, usually all on the same piece of content, from different angles.
